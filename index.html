About Me

I am a passionate Computer Science student with a lifelong enthusiasm for programming and technology. My journey began in elementary school when I discovered "Command Blocks" in Minecraft. This unique system served as my introduction to logical thinking and programming logic, allowing me to build interactive mini-games and manage small servers for a local gaming community. That early experience sparked a deep curiosity in me, pushing me to explore what else I could create through code.

Driven by this curiosity, I started learning visual programming with Scratch. However, I quickly hit its technical limitations and decided to take a leap into text-based coding with C#. Initially, the complexity of C# was overwhelming, but I persevered, learning how to write basic text outputs and calculation logic. Later, I discovered Python, which felt incredibly intuitive. Utilizing its rich library ecosystem, I successfully developed classic games like Tic-Tac-Toe and Snake, which strengthened my understanding of fundamental software logic. During high school, I continued to expand my knowledge by exploring JavaScript and building various small-scale projects.

Looking toward the future, my primary career goal is to become a skilled Software Developer or Game Developer. I am highly interested in pursuing roles in Backend or Full Stack development, and I hold a special passion for the game development industry. Currently, my technical interests are focused on writing Minecraft server plugins using Java, as well as experimenting with mini-computers like Raspberry Pi and Arduino. I am actively exploring how to utilize these hardware boards to host and run the web applications I am developing. I am confident that my lifelong dedication to coding, combined with my eagerness to learn, makes me a strong candidate for a Web Developer internship.
